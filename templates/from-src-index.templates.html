Goal:

help to make spending money more harmonic (in common sense)

Aim:

× help to understand balance in every jug after income and before each income

Algorithm

  1. calculate expense (common)
    expense (common) = end_balance (common) - (initial_balance (common) + income (common))
  2. define expense by jugs (manually)
  3. calculate end balance by jugs
    end_balance (by jugs) = init_balance (by jugs) + income (by jugs) - expense (by jugs)
